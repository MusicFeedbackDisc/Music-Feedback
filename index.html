Welcome to Music Feedback!

Daily Events | Featured Artist of the Week | Music Lessons


Music Feedback is a Discord community built on the foundation of sharing industry experience and expertise. We help musicians further their careers and hobbies through critical and supportive advice while providing opportunities to exemplify improvement and understanding.

The server is home to over 4,000 musicians, featuring a large selection of Electronic, Alternative, and Rap music. The server was created in July 2020 and has since hosted two digital festivals, MF Fest 2021 and MF Fest 2022, available for viewing on our YouTube page.








Who We Are

Jenn | Jenn#0779
Founder

Jenn is a classically trained musician with 15 years of trumpet experience and 2 years of vocal experience. She has worked in ensemble and band settings and earned her Bachelor of Science in Music.

Jenn has experience creating content for and managing social media, recruiting and contacting for A&R, as well as researching and implementing PR campaigns for musical artists. She has practiced lyricism, songwriting, and production over the past 5 years and currently works as a barback at a local music venue.

She offers insight and guidance for managing music releases, press and promotion, and critical listening for production, songwriting, and lyricism.

Dan | MakiMaki#0653
Admin

Dan first stepped foot on stage over 20 years ago. He studied vocal performance at the Musician’s Institute in Hollywood, CA and worked under late platinum songwriter/producer, Jim Huff (Joe Bonamassa - “Dirt In My Pocket”).

Though primarily a vocalist, Dan plays multiple instruments and is a seasoned guitarist. He is both the guitarist and second vocalist for the experimental rock project, AWES.

Dan works as a traveling festival bartender, enjoys camping, poker, and spending time with good friends. Dan derives most of his abilities from his awesome hair, which has been scientifically confirmed to power Manhattan for 7.5 years.
